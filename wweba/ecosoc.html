ECO Clubs
  1. Home
  2. Initiatives
  3. ECO Clubs

Initiated by the Ministry of Environment and Forests, Government of India. it is a   programme to sensitize school children about environment, its problems and conservation. Started in the year 2001, the programme is operational across the country   through school eco-clubs established for this purpose.

ACTIVITIES OF ECO-CLUB SCHOOLS

The teachers of ecoclub schools are given an orientation programme along with cash amount of Rs.2500, a calendar and activity diary per school. The main activities done by eco clubs are to understand and create awareness of biodiversity conservation and local environment, creation clean and green consciousness among students through various innovative methods.
