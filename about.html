HIERARCHY

The Division is Headed by the Divisional Forest Officer (DFO) and Range & Beats by the Incharges ie ACFs or Forest Rangers.

Range

There are 5 Range under the Division namely:
          Dimapur Range: Presently headed by an Asst. Conservator of Forests. There are two Forest Check gates under Dimapur Range.
          Chumukedima Range: Headed by an Asst. Conservator of Forests. There is one Forest Check gate .
          Niuland Range: Headed by an Asst. Conservator of Forests. There is one Forest Check gate .
          Kuhuboto Range: Headed by an Asst. Conservator of Forests.
          Rangapahar Range: Headed by a Forest Ranger.


Beats

There are two Independent beats under the Division, namely,
          Medziphema Beat: Headed by Forest Ranger.
          Piphema Beat: Headed by Deputy Ranger.
          (NB: the List of officers , staffs and contact may be viewed in the RTI section)
