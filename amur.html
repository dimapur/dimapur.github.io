Ecotourism on Amur Falcon Bird Watching in Dimapur

Dimapur is the ancient capital of DimasaKachari tribal rulers who existed before thirteenth century AD and is now the largest city of Nagaland. It is also known as the ‘gateway of Nagaland’ .Magnificent waterfalls, ancient villages, ancient Kali temple and ruins of the early existing Kachari kingdom are the main Dimapur attractions. Dimapur is also fortunate to be one of the roosting site of the Amu falcons, during its peak time one can view several lakhs of this migratory bird which is a breath takingview. Dimapur is well connected by road, rail and flight .

About Great migration:

The Amur falcon is a small raptor of the falcon family. It breeds in south-eastern Siberia and Northern China before migrating in large flocks across India and over the Arabian Sea to winter in Southern Africa. In 2013 in a conservation effort, Naga, Pangti and Wokha, three Amur falcons were fitted with satellite chips in Nagaland to track their migration route that was unknown to ornithologists and now The entire migration route of Amur falcons from Nagaland to South Africa, Mongolia and back to Nagaland has been scientifically plotted.

In Dimapur, these migratory falcons roosts in the Teak Plantations in and around Niuland and Kuhuboto Ranges under Dimapur Forest Division which is about 30 kms away from Dimapur main town and about 20 KM from Airport.

Unique nature of Amur Falcon bird watching:

Amur falcon is unique in many ways. It comes in hundreds of thousands. Skies will be full of single species. It will be a visual treat and one cannot forget it for their lifetime.

  • Arrival dates in Dimapur forest division:

    The Amur Falcons arrive by end of October and leave on November last week. For exact details on arrivals which varies year to year, Send us a mail at dfodimapur@yahoo.com

  • Ecotourism on Bird watching:

    As the name suggests, Ecotourism involves tourism without harming the nature. Bird watching is a popular hobby among wild life enthusiasts around the world. The satisfaction that bird watching gives for bird watchers is immense and only those who experience it can tell further about it.Bird watching is an ideal way to keep in touch with nature, and you also have the satisfaction of learning something new. Birding also relieves stress and can provide a place of solitude except for the sweet song of a bird.

  • Bird watching in Dimapur:

    Dimapur being a well connected city in north east India, It has a great potential to become the bird watching capital of North east India. Dimapur Forest division is promoting ecotourism on Amur falcon bird watching through online platforms from 2016.

How to reach Dimapur?

One can find great places of accommodation in Dimapur and get advanced bookings. Tourists can find several options for how to reach Dimapur.

Train

Dimapur has decent railways connectivity with various Indian cities. Trains such as Jan Shatabdi connect Dimapur with various North Eastern cities. Brahmaputra mail and Rajdhani express are some famously known trains which connect Dimapur with distant North Indian cities like New Delhi. There are 5 to 6 trains daily plying from Guwahati to Dimapur.

Flight

Dimapur airport is the only airport in Nagaland. Regular flights connect this airport with major Indian cities. There are two flights daily and a direct flight to New Delhi .

Road

Dimapur has good condition of roads and many private buses operate on various routes connecting Dimapur with nearby cities.
